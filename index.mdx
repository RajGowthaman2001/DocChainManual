**DocChain** is a cutting-edge trust infrastructure that uses blockchain technology to securely issue, verify, and track documents. As one of the largest blockchain-based platforms for document security worldwide, DocChain has issued over **58 million documents** and safeguarded financial assets worth over **$3 billion.**

Developed by **Print2Block Inc.,** DocChain is trusted by government institutions and large enterprises to ensure document integrity and security.
