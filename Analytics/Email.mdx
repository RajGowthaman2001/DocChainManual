## Overview

- Here, you can view the counts of email,

  - **Sent** email

  - **Delivered** email

  - **Bounced** email

  - **Opened** email

- Below the stats, you will see a chart that visually represents the statistical data of your email performance.

- You can also view the detailed email log list, which tracks individual email statuses and outcomes. Hover over the email icon in the respective list to see the email status.
