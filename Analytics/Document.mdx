## Overview

- Here, you can view the counts of **Issued, Viewed, Verified** and **Downloaded** documents.

- Below, you will see a chart representing the statistical data.

- You can also view lists of **Viewed, Verified** and **Downloaded** documents.

- To change the displayed document type, click on the **Viewed, Verified** or **Downloaded** tabs.

- To access **Single Document Analytics**, click on the respective list.

- To view the location information of a document, click the **Location Info** button in the respective list.
