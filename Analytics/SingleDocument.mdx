## Overview

- Here, you can view the counts of Viewed, Verified, and Downloaded actions for the single document.

- Below the stats, you will find the Document View page URL along with a QR Code containing the URL. You can copy and share this URL via social media.

- Further down, you can see the logs for actions related to the single document, including:

  - **Viewed** actions

  - **Verified** actions

  - **Downloaded** actions

- To change the displayed document type, click on the Viewed, Verified or Downloaded tabs.

- To view the location information of a document, click the Location Info button in the respective list.

- You can also view the document’s email log list. Hover over the email icon in the respective list to see the email status.
